Women deserve equal rights. Women are human, too.

What are women’s rights?

Women rights are human rights. They are the fundamental rights that gives us the right to live free from violence, slavery and discrimination. They are the rights that gives us the right to education, own property, vote, and earn fair and equal wage.

Don’t Women Already Have Rights?

Yes, women do have rights, but women also lack many rights. Some of these rights include rights to fair pay, equal job opportunities, freedom from violence, and much more.
