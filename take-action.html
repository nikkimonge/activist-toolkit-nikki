Action

TAKE. ACTION.

The government needs to pass legislation to better protect women. This should include enforcing equal pay, equal job opportunities, stricter sexual abuse laws, more sexual abuse support, rights to make our own choices for our bodies, and more. To make this happen, we need you to follow these steps. Find your representatives and contact them. Demand a change from them. If you are old enough, register to vote. It is important that we have leaders that will fight for us, not against us. And spread awareness!
